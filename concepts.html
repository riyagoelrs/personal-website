TAPÉ concept · pre-order view the brand →

one dispenser. every formula.

buy the device once. pop in whichever formula tape your skin needs that day — hydration, glow, firming, clarifying, or calm. like a great razor: invest in the tool once, then just keep replacing what runs out.

the bet: skincare is the last category still selling you a new bottle every time the formula changes. tapé separates the hardware from the refill, and lets your routine flex to your skin instead of your shelf.
