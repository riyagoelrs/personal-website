About the brand

i'm the founder of indiya nyc. i source block prints from jaipur ateliers, design the cuts, write the copy, and ship the orders. every piece is small-batch, traceable to the artisan who made it, and built to outlast the season — not the algorithm.

we're not a brand built on volume. we're a brand built on the slowest, oldest hand in the room.
